Latitude vs. Wind Speed


This scatter plot shows how temperature changes as latitude approaches zero, that is, as we approach the equator. As expected, we can see how temperatures are normally distributed, peaking at, or near the equator. When looking at the chart, we must keep in mind that it is a snapshot reflecting only one day's data.
