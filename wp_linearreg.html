Does North or South Matter?

Since we collected all this great weather data from cities all over the world, and we finished all our comparisons, we started to wonder whether weather tendencies with relation to the equator differ from Northern to Southern hemisphere. If we separated the Earth into its upper and lower halves, would the same patterns emerge, or would hemisphere play a role?
