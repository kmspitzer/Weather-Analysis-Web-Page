Weather Where You Are

How Does Weather Change As We Approach the Equator?


Sure, we all learned as kids that temperatures are highest at or near the equator. But just how is weather affected by proximity to the equator? Does weather phenomena in the Northern Hemisphere differ from those in the Southern Hemiphere?

We randomly generated latitude and longitude values to pinpoint more than 500 cities around the world, and collected weather data from the Open Weather API. We used this weather data to take a closer look at how weather conditions change as we approach the equator.
